Project
Stephen
Burks


Stephen Burks is one of the most recognized American Industrial Designers. He is the first African-American designer to collaborate with renowned international brands and a ‘Design Activist’ supporting the developing world trough art.

Being a big fan of his work and activism, I wanted to recreate a site that would showcase his incredible work in a clean, simple and creative way. My goal was to use his artwork to section the layout and achieve simplicity with a touch of luxury.

It is fully responsive, hand coded from scratch with full-screen sections using the 100% height of the viewport. I used Bootstrap for parts of the site, and media queries to support the responsiveness of the custom sections.
